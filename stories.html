CODE 11.59
BY AUDEMARS PIGUET

The Code 11.59 Minute Repeater Supersonnerie
is a simple three-hand watch with a small seconds
at 6 o'clock. It pairs a blue Grand Feu enamel dial
with an 18-karat white gold case. A lever on the case's
left-hand side operates the minute repeater.
